Reports: Quarterly Emissions- Facility Level View

  Print Help
If an emitting source has not reported emissions for all units for the specified period, the Reporting Status column will contain an "Incomplete." If the Reporting Status column contains an "Incomplete" but emissions data is present, the emitting source has reported data for a portion of the period or for at least one unit at a multi-unit facility.

The Reporting Status column on the quarterly emissions report will contain "Incomplete" until after the corresponding quarter ends. Emitting sources must report emissions for a quarter within thirty days following the end of the quarter covered by the report.

Sources for which no emissions were reported due to unit operating status as reported to the U.S. EPA's CAMD Business System are considered not operating. Sources that were not operating as of January 1, 2012 and are not anticipated to operate going forward are not considered CO2 budget sources for the second control period (January 1, 2012 through December 31, 2014). Should this unit resume operation in the future, the operating status would be reconsidered at that time. Not Operating sources have been removed from the RGGI COATS Sources report.

Emissions data provided in the RGGI COATS report may not include all emitting units or emissions for a specific time period due to pending data processing or resolution of technical source reporting issues. The RGGI COATS report indicates whether the reporting status is complete or incomplete. Additionally, emissions data in RGGI COATS may be updated to reflect the correction of technical issues upon resubmission of data by source representatives.

Note: New Jersey withdrew its agreement to the RGGI MOU effective January 1, 2012. Emissions from New Jersey sources are reported for the first control period only (January 1, 2009 - December 31, 2011).
